How much fun can it really be?

Web site contains examples of fun things to try while on an elevator:

  1. Blow your nose and offer to show the contents of your Kleenex to other passengers.
  2. Whistle the first seven notes of "It's Small World" incessantly.
  3. Stand silent and motionless in the conmer, facing the wall, without getting off.
  4. Frown and muter "gotta go, gotta go" then sight and say "oops!"
  5. Say "Dings!" at each floor.
